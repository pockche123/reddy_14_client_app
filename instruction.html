Instruction

In this game your teacher will be able to assess your geography knowledge! You have to answer as many capital country questions as possible in the given time! The classroom scores will then be displayed on the leaderboard from highest to lowest. Please follow the instructions below. Once you start the test you won't be able to see them again.

  • Click on the 'Play' button;
  • Select the difficult level depending on how confident you feel about the topic you have three options: Easy: 3min/Medium: 2min/Hard: 1min;
  • A country flag and its respective country name will be displayed;
  • You have two options: To either pass a question or type and submit your answer;
  • To submit your answer press the 'Enter' button otherwise press 'Pass';
  • As you keep playing you will be able to keep track of your score;
  • Once the timer stops you will be forwarded to the leaderboard page.
